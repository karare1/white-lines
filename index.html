Welcome to the WHITE LINES
Tennis Academy

Step on Court with confidence

ALL AGES, ALL LEVELS, ALL WELCOME


WHITE LINES Tennis Academy is located in Penarth, a small welsh town near Cardiff, in a picaresque and quiet area of The Penarth Pier. It offers tennis camps and professional programs for children from 5 years old, older juniors and adults who seek to start or improve their tennis level and obtain results. Whether you're an experienced player or a complete beginner, we've got everything you need to improve your game — from professional coaching to competitive events. The academy has 10 professional courts, 7 of red clay and 3 of synthetic resin.

We make sure our players enjoy the journey of improvement so that they can compete at the level they aspire to.

We focus on personalized approach to reach best results for each player.

We love the spirit of competition. The training programs are designed with the goal of raising the tennis game of the players by creating a competitive environment.

WHAT WE OFFER

WHITE LINES MAIN PROGRAMMES

Yellow group:
Small Children (Age 5-7)

This is an introduction to tennis. Here, we’ll help kids improve their balance, agility and co-ordination while teaching them about the sport we love. At this stage it’s not about winning or losing, it’s just getting to grips with the basics.

Learn More

Orange group:
Juniors (Age 8-10)

This will look a bit more like the tennis you know. You’ll see overarm serves, rallies and volleys. Coaches will help youth to further develop their co-ordination, balance and speed. We’ll introduce relaxed competition here too.

Learn More

Red group:
Older Juniors (Age 11-13)

It’s time for youth to play on full size courts. Now it’s all about growing their game by ﬁne tuning and reﬁning the techniques they’ve learned so far. Youth develop a smarter game, working out how they can beat their oponent.

Learn More

Green group:
Teenagers (Age 14-17)

Time to find your game. Playing on a full size court with the same size balls the pros use, youth will continue to explore different styles on the court, and start to choose their own. They will develop a lifelong love of the game.

Learn More

Adults:
(Age 18 and over) - 3 programmes

Beginners, Intermediate, Advanced.
Our adult tennis classes suit either those picking up a racket for the first time or those who want to hone their skills. We’ll help you make the most of your tennis game.

Learn More

Private lessons:
suitable for all individuals

More tailored approach. Use the individual lessons to work with an instructor to improve your tennis playing ability. They will analyse your playing style and help you work on the part of your game that need the most improvement.

Learn More
